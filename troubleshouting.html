ImageTop Troubleshooting Guide

1. Prevent Auto-Start While Playing Video

To prevent the ImageTop app from automatically starting while you are playing a video, you can disable the Auto (Inactivity) Start feature. Simply click on the ImageTop icon in the top bar menu and select "Disable Auto Start".

2. Starting the App at Startup

To have the ImageTop app start automatically when you boot up your Mac, you can add the app to your startup items. Go to System Preferences > Users & Groups, select your user account, click on "Login Items", and then add ImageTop to the list.

3. Refreshing Pexel Photos and Videos

If you need to refresh the Pexel photos and videos displayed by the app, you can easily do so by unselecting and then reselecting the appropriate options in the settings. Access the settings from the ImageTop icon in the top bar menu.
